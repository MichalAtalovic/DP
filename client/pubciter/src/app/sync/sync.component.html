Synchronization
Synchronization process searches for your publications on Google Scholar and retrieves its citations. Be aware that if you want to sychronize citations from SEMANTICS SCHOLAR or WWW.OPENCITATIONS.NET, you need to provided your publications with the DOI (Digital object identifier) manually, then re-synchronize your library. To review current synchronization settings, please visit the "Settings" section in the main menu.
